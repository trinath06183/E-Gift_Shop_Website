Our Story

At Lumina Gifts, we celebrate India's rich cultural heritage through meticulously curated gifts that tell a story. Each piece in our collection is handpicked from master artisans across India, representing centuries of tradition and craftsmanship.

From the intricate brass work of Moradabad to the delicate pashminas of Kashmir, from the marble sculptures of Jaipur to the silver artistry of Rajasthan – we bring you authentic treasures that embody luxury, elegance, and cultural significance.

We believe that the perfect gift is more than just an object; it's an emotion, a memory, and a connection between hearts. Let us help you create unforgettable moments with gifts that truly matter.
